Meowscular Chef's Special Lagi Tail Sushi

Description

Eat a high protein meal with Meowscular Chef's Lagi Tail Sushi!!! The perfect pre-hunt meal to get you feeling strong and energized to take down your next big game monster! This meal is packed with electrifying protein to get you feeling awake and in shape! Lagiacrus meat is packed with a high energy electrical element and tons of Vitamind D. Also a low-fat meal so if you are on a cutting diet This meal is perfect.

Ingredients:

  • Lagiacrus Tail
  • Ridgemane Tuna
  • Osseoctopus
  • Kut-ku bean
  • Sunkissed Grass
  • Jewel Cactus

Steps:

  1. Chopp The Lagiacrus tail into diced cubes that are perfectly symmtrical. They have to be perfectly symmetrical otherwise the recipe is garabagg
  2. Skin the Tuna and save the scales for later as we will turn them in scaled whetstones as they will be useful on your next hunt.
  3. Release the Osseoctopus back to the ocean as it is good luck to release them for the next hunt
  4. season the Kut-ku beans with whatever spices you like. The higher the protein the better!
  5. Take your sunkissed grass clean them under a strong waterfall, the journey to the waterfall will make you stronger so it's good practice. Dice em up and sprinkle them on the tail as you sautee it.
  6. Open up the Jewel Cactus just drink the liquid inside.
  7. wrap it up in a nice seaweed wrap then dice and serve! Good luck on your next hunt!
