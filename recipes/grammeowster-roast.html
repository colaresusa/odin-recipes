Grammeowster Chef's Deviljho Sausage Soup

Description

Grammeowster Chef's Deviljho sausage is the most heartwarming and homey meal you can eat to relax after a grueling and difficult hunt. You would think since the meat is that of a Deviljho it would be tough and chewy but this recipe makes it soft and tender. The soup will warm and hunter and make them have a restul nap afterwards.

Ingredients:

  • Deviljho Tail
  • Sleep herbs
  • Dragonbloom
  • Cudgel Onion
  • plumpkin

Steps:

  1. Tenderize the Tail so its soft enough to put throught the grinder
  2. Grind the Tail into sausage links and then proceeed to chop the links evenly, first into whole cirles then into quarters.
  3. Start Boiling the Herbs and onions in a pot, add some spices prefferably some fire herbs on hand if you have any.
  4. Dice the plumpkin up along with the dragonbloom
  5. Toss those 2 Ingredients together in a bowl to serve on the side
  6. At this point add the sausages to the pot and wait 57 min exactly and stirring for 5 min exactly with EXACTLY 2 min breaks in between.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
