Ichiraku Ramen

Description

Ichiraku Ramen is the favorite food of the famous hokage NARUTO UZUMAKI. After saving the leaf village countless times the hokage prefers filling his stomach with this famous beef brothed ramen. It's delicous, savory and will most likely be your last meal before you head out on your dangerous mission!

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • Chopped green onions
  • Noodles
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Make your Soy sauce real nice.
  2. Cook your noodles real nice.
  3. Cook your Steak real nice.
  4. Cook your eggs real nice.
  5. Mix everything real nice.
  6. Enjoy real nice.
